Al-Noor Lasani MDF

MDF is a versatile board derived from sustainable timbers bonded together with synthetic resins under high temperatures. Smoother than plywood and denser than chipboard,
MDF is widely used in the wood working industry. Al-Noor Lasani is available in a wide range of thickness and can be used in a variety of applications
Also available in HMR (High Moisture Resistance) in 17mm thickness.
